## Miss Mary Mack / 瑪麗馬克小姐
### 1.
```
Miss Mary Mack, Mack, Mack.
All dressed in black, black, black.
With silver buckles, buckles, buckles.
Up and down her back, back, back.
```
```

上下跳動在她的背後，背後，背後。
```
